Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

At Zonofal, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email) if you reach out via our contact form
  • Usage data (browser type, device, pages visited, time spent) via analytics tools
  • Cookies and local storage for language preference, session behavior, and UI settings
  • Technical data (IP address, operating system) for security and diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and provide support
  • To improve site performance, accessibility, and user experience
  • To personalize content based on language, region, or device
  • To monitor security and detect unauthorized access or misuse

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We store your data securely using encrypted systems and access controls. We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • You may opt out of non-essential cookies and analytics tracking
  • You may request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • You may withdraw consent for data processing at any time

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (e.g., analytics, video hosting, form processors) that collect data independently. These providers have their own privacy policies and terms of service. We recommend reviewing them before interacting with embedded content.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated on this page. Continued use of Zonofal after updates implies acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this policy, contact us via our form or email privacy@zonofal.org

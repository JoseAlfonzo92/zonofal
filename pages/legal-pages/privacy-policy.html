Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

At Zonofal, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what limited data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email) if you reach out via our contact form
  • Basic technical data (IP address, browser type, operating system) for security and diagnostics
  • Local storage used strictly for essential preferences such as language and accessibility settings (no analytics, advertising, or tracking)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and provide support
  • To maintain site functionality, accessibility, and security
  • To remember your language and accessibility preferences using local storage

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We store your data securely using encrypted systems and access controls. We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. We do not sell, share, or use your data for marketing or profiling.

4. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • You may request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Where applicable, you may withdraw consent for data processing at any time

5. Third-Party Services

Zonofal does not embed third-party services that set cookies or track users.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this policy, contact us via our form or email zonofal@gmail.com

Zonofal is built with a privacy-first mindset, focusing on simplicity, accessibility, and user trust.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Zonofal does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. We respect your privacy and ensure that your browsing experience on our site is free from cookie-based tracking.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Since Zonofal does not use cookies, no such files are stored when you browse our site.

2. Our Approach to Privacy

We believe in a privacy-first design. This means we avoid unnecessary tracking, do not profile visitors, and do not sell or share personal data. Any information you provide (such as through contact forms) is used solely to respond to your request.

3. Third-Party Services

Zonofal does not embed third-party services (such as analytics, ads, or social media plugins) that set cookies. If you follow external links from our site, please review the cookie and privacy policies of those providers.

4. Essential Storage

Zonofal may use minimal, essential browser storage strictly for functional purposes, such as remembering language preferences. This storage is not used for analytics, advertising, tracking, or profiling, and does not monitor behavior across websites, sessions, or devices.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to browse Zonofal without being tracked. You also have the right to request deletion of any personal information you provide through forms or communications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our cookie-free policy or privacy practices, feel free to contact us at zonofal@gmail.com

Zonofal is built with a privacy-first mindset, focusing on simplicity, accessibility, and user trust.
